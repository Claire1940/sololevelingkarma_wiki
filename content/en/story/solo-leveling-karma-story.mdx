<Section icon={Map} title="Solo Leveling Karma Story: The Untold 27-Year War Canon">

The narrative of **Solo Leveling: KARMA** explores one of the most requested and unexplored periods in the entire franchise: the 27-year war waged by Sung Jinwoo in the Dimensional Gap. Following the events of the original webtoon, Jinwoo uses the Cup of Reincarnation to turn back time, taking the entire burden of the war against the Monarchs onto himself. He steps into the Dimensional Gap to intercept the Monarchs before they can reach Earth, initiating a fierce, lonely conflict that spans nearly three decades. 

This canonical story was developed under the close supervision of series creator Chugong, ensuring that every narrative beat aligns with official lore. While Jinwoo experiences 27 years of brutal combat, only two years pass in the real world. 

<Callout type="tip" title="Narrative Significance">
The manhwa's original ending felt rushed to many fans because this massive 27-year war was condensed into a brief epilogue. This game serves as the official expansion of that lost era, making it a critical piece of the franchise's canon.
</Callout>

| Story Pillar | Lore Details | Impact on Sung Jinwoo |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Dimensional Gap** | A war-torn, unstable realm between dimensions where time flows differently. | Forces Jinwoo to fight constantly without respite. |
| **Power Dispersion** | Jinwoo is forced to disperse his powers to survive interdimensional travel. | He begins the game weakened, justifying the roguelite progression. |
| **The Monarchs' Alliance** | Led by Antares, the Monarch of Destruction, who is voiced by Troy Baker. | Creates a strategic, high-stakes battle against organized cosmic threats. |
| **Mutated Hunters** | Twisted beings created by the Monarchs that wear the faces of people Jinwoo knows. | Inflicts severe emotional distress and psychological warfare on Jinwoo. |
