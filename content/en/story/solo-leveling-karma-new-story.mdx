The upcoming roguelite action RPG by Netmarble, Solo Leveling: KARMA, introduces an original, fully canonical narrative that expands the universe like never before. The **solo leveling karma new story** focuses entirely on the unexplored 27-year war fought by Sung Jinwoo inside the Dimensional Gap. While this massive conflict was only briefly referenced and largely skipped in the original webtoon and light novel, the game's narrative brings this crucial era to the forefront. 

Because time flows differently within the Dimensional Gap, Sung Jinwoo endures nearly three decades of continuous battle, whereas only two years pass in the real world. This makes the game's story a direct, canonical bridge that connects the events of the original series to the sequel, Solo Leveling: Ragnarok.

<Section icon={Swords} title="A Shattered Monarch: Jinwoo's Power Dispersion">

As Sung Jinwoo enters the Dimensional Gap using the Cup of Reincarnation, he faces unexpected physical and spiritual consequences. Traveling through the interdimensional Gates forces the Shadow Monarch to disperse a portion of his overwhelming power to survive the journey. This narrative device explains why Jinwoo begins his journey in a weakened state, aligning perfectly with the game's progression systems.

Throughout the campaign, players must help Jinwoo reclaim his lost strength, master new weapon types, and gather randomized "Blessings" to survive increasingly hostile environments.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Mutated Hunters: Who Are They?">

One of the most tragic and compelling additions to the **solo leveling karma new story** is the introduction of the **Mutated Hunters** (also referred to as Corrupted Hunters). These entities are original adversaries engineered specifically by the Monarchs to exploit Jinwoo's psychological weaknesses and eliminate him. 

The Monarchs have created these monstrous beings to mimic the physical appearances and combat styles of the human Hunters Jinwoo fought alongside in his past life. This forces Jinwoo into a deeply emotional conflict as he is forced to strike down monsters wearing the faces of people he once knew and protected.
