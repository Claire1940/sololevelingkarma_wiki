The Blessing system serves as the primary roguelite progression mechanic in **solo leveling karma**. Instead of relying on static character builds, each run presents randomized Blessing upgrades that alter your stats, enhance your skills, and dictate your combat strategy.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Shadow Army Summoning Mechanics">

No Solo Leveling game is complete without the Shadow Army. In **solo leveling karma**, Sung Jinwoo can summon loyal Shadow Soldiers to fight alongside him in real-time, providing both offensive support and tactical utility.

- **Igris**: The loyal knight returns as a high-mobility melee companion. He excels at executing rapid slash attacks and initiating signature combo maneuvers alongside Jinwoo.
- **Iron**: This massive tank unit is perfect for crowd control. Iron draws enemy aggro, absorbs heavy hits, and staggers large groups of Magic Beasts.
- **Shadow Combos**: Certain Blessings allow you to trigger joint attacks. When timed correctly, summoning a Shadow during a weapon-switch animation unleashes a devastating battlefield-sweeping strike.

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Summoning">
Do not summon your entire Shadow Army at once. Save Iron for phases where bosses execute wide-area attacks to help draw attention, and unleash Igris when the boss is staggered to maximize your damage window.
</Callout>

</Section>
