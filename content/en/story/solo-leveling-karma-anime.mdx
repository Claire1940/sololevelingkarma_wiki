<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Roguelite Progression">

The gameplay of Solo Leveling: KARMA departs from the traditional linear ARPG structure. Instead, it adopts a **Roguelite** framework. Every time you enter a dungeon or a segment of the Dimensional Gap, the layout, enemy spawns, and available power-ups change. This ensures that the **solo leveling karma anime** experience remains fresh, challenging players to adapt their strategies on the fly.

A typical run involves clearing "Gates" or stages, where players collect **Blessings**—randomized buffs that enhance specific attributes or skills. If you fall in battle, you return to the central hub, retaining some permanent currency to upgrade Jinwoo’s base stats, but losing the temporary Blessings gained during that specific run.

<Callout type="warning" title="Survival Strategy">
In Roguelite games, survival is often better than speed. Prioritize defensive Blessings early in your run to ensure you reach the final boss of the sector.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Weapons and Shadow Army">

Combat in Solo Leveling: KARMA is fast-paced and emphasizes **Weapon Switching**. Unlike other RPGs where you are locked into a single class, Jinwoo can equip multiple weapons and cycle through them during a combo. This allows for devastating synergy between close-range melee and long-range pressure.

The **Shadow Army** acts as a tactical extension of your character. You don't just "summon and forget"; you command them to perform specific combo attacks. For instance, Igris can be summoned to execute a vertical strike that launches enemies, allowing Jinwoo to follow up with a mid-air dagger flurry.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy">
Switching from a Heavy Sword to Dual Daggers immediately after a successful parry triggers a "Karma Burst," significantly increasing your critical hit rate for 5 seconds.
</Callout>
