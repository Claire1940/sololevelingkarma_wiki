<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and the Shadow Army">

Combat in Solo Leveling: KARMA is fast-paced and emphasizes the "Action" in Action RPG. Unlike traditional MMOs, the roguelite nature means that every "run" starts with a baseline but evolves through the acquisition of skills and allies. The most iconic feature is the **Shadow Army**. You don't fight alone; you summon the shadows of defeated enemies to control the battlefield.

The synergy between Sung Jinwoo’s direct attacks and his summons is the core of the gameplay loop. For instance, summoning **Igris** can trigger combo attacks that deal massive break damage to bosses, while **Iron** provides defensive utility and crowd control.

**Key Combat Pillars:**
- **Weapon Switching**: Seamlessly swap between daggers, longswords, and scythes to adapt to enemy resistances.
- **Shadow Summons**: Call upon the Shadow Army to perform signature moves and support abilities.
- **Combo Skills**: Chain player attacks with Shadow Soldier abilities for devastating finishers.

| Shadow Unit | Combat Role | Signature Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Igris** | Melee DPS | High-speed sword flurry / Break damage |
| **Iron** | Tank / CC | Shield bash / Taunt enemies |
| **Shadow Infantry** | Fodder / Distraction | Swarm tactics / Constant pressure |
| **Original Shadows** | Tactical Support | Game-specific utility skills |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Combat Tip">
Mastering the timing of your weapon switches is vital. Some bosses have "Armor" bars that can only be effectively depleted by heavy weapons like hammers before you switch to daggers for rapid damage.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Blessing System: Roguelite Progression">

The **Blessing System** is what defines the "Karma" in the game's title. During a run, players are presented with randomized Blessings that alter Sung Jinwoo’s attributes, skill effects, and Shadow Army behavior. This ensures that no two playthroughs of the same dungeon feel identical.

By combining different Blessings, you can create specialized builds. For example, a "Shadow Commander" build focuses on maximizing the damage and frequency of your summons, while a "Lone Monarch" build might sacrifice summon power for massive self-buffs to Jinwoo’s physical speed and power.

| Blessing Tier | Effect Strength | Rarity | Typical Use Case |

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start Your First Run">

Starting your journey in Solo Leveling: KARMA requires understanding the roguelite loop. You will begin at the "Dimensional Hub," where you can prepare your gear and select your starting Shadow Soldiers before diving into the rifts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Loadout">
    Choose two primary weapons and your starting Shadow Soldiers. Early on, a balance of a fast weapon (Daggers) and a heavy weapon (Greatsword) is recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Rift">
    Progress through procedurally generated floors. Each floor contains enemies, hidden chests, and a Blessing altar at the end.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Your Blessings">
    After clearing a room, select one of three randomized Blessings. Always look for synergies with your current weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Floor Boss">
    Every few floors, you will face a mini-boss. Defeating them grants permanent currency used for meta-progression outside the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Meta-Progression">
    Use collected resources in the Hub to permanently upgrade Jinwoo’s base stats, ensuring future runs are easier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Death is Not the End">
As a roguelite, dying in a run is expected. You will lose your temporary Blessings, but you keep the "Karma Essence" needed for permanent upgrades.
</Callout>

</Section>
