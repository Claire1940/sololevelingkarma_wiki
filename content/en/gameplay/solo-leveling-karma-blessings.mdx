<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding the Blessings System">

The **solo leveling karma blessings** system is a core progression mechanic in Solo Leveling: KARMA. Blessings act as powerful passive modifiers that players can equip to customize their playstyle, boost overall combat power, and overcome challenging boss encounters. Unlike standard gear, blessings offer scaling percentage bonuses and unique tactical triggers that can completely alter the flow of battle.

To maximize your team's efficiency, you must understand how blessings integrate with your active hunters and weapons. Each blessing occupies a specific slot, and players can unlock additional slots as they progress through the main campaign and level up their account. Choosing the right combination of blessings allows you to fine-tune your stats, such as critical hit rate, attack speed, and elemental damage.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Blessings">

Upgrading your blessings is essential to keep pace with the escalating difficulty of late-game content. The upgrade process requires specific materials that can be farmed daily. Follow this step-by-step process to efficiently upgrade your blessings without wasting valuable resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Blessing Menu">
    Navigate to the character screen and select the Blessings tab. Here you can view all currently unlocked blessings, their current levels, and equipped slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Upgrade Materials">
    Acquire Blessing Powder and Gold from daily gates, weekly raids, and shop purchases. High-tier upgrades will also require duplicate blessing cards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Upgrade">
    Select the target blessing and confirm the upgrade. Note that higher levels require more resources and have varying success scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Test">
    Equip the upgraded blessing to your active slot and test its performance in the training area or a low-level gate to ensure it fits your rotation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
