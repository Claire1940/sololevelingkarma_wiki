The upgrade path consists of leveling up, limit breaking, and slotting blessings. Limit breaking is particularly important as it raises the level cap of your weapon, unlocking higher base stats and stronger passive skills.

  <Step num="1" title="Acquisition">

  <Step num="3" title="Limit Break">
