<Section icon={Target} title="How to Setup Crossplay Progress">

Setting up your **solo leveling karma crossplay** profile is a straightforward process, but it must be done correctly during the initial launch to avoid losing any progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create a Netmarble Account">
    Visit the official Netmarble website or use the in-game prompt to create a master account. This will act as the hub for all your save data.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Link Your First Device">
    Launch the game on your primary platform (e.g., PC). Log in with your Netmarble ID and complete the tutorial to establish your initial save file.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enable Cloud Sync">
    Go to the Settings menu and ensure that "Cloud Save" or "Account Sync" is toggled to 'On'. This ensures every mission completion is uploaded.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Log in on Secondary Devices">
    Download the game on your mobile device. On the title screen, select 'Log In' instead of 'Guest Play' and enter your Netmarble credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Verify Data Integrity">
    Once logged in, check your inventory and Shadow Army to confirm that all items from your PC session have successfully transferred.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Security Warning">
Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your Netmarble account. Since your account holds all your progress across multiple platforms, keeping it secure is vital.
</Callout>
