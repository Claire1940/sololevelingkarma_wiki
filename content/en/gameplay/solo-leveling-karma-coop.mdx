### Boss Strategy: The Twin Statues
One of the most iconic co-op encounters involves the Twin Statues. This fight requires the team to split focus. While the Tank keeps the aggressive statue occupied, the DPS must focus on the casting statue to prevent a map-wide fire blast. Communication is key here; use the "Focus Fire" ping to coordinate targets.

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Strategy">
Wait for the boss's "Break" state before unleashing all Ultimate abilities. Damage is increased by 200% during this window, making it the most efficient time to burn through health bars.
</Callout>
