<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering the Roguelite Loop">

Starting your journey in **solo leveling karma pc** requires a shift in mindset from traditional RPGs. Because the game utilizes a roguelite structure, death is not the end but a part of the progression cycle. Each run provides "Karma" or similar resources that allow you to upgrade Sung Jinwoo's permanent stats, making subsequent attempts easier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Run">
    Select your starting weapon and enter the Dimensional Gap. Your initial stats are determined by your permanent upgrades, but your active skills will be gathered during the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Combat Chambers">
    Navigate through branching paths. Some rooms offer Blessings, others provide weapon upgrades, and some contain elite Magic Beasts. Choosing the right path is crucial for build synergy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Blessings">
    After clearing a wave, you will be presented with random Blessings. These are temporary buffs that define your current run's playstyle, such as increased crit for daggers or lifesteal for greatswords.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Floor Boss">
    Every major segment ends with a boss encounter like Cerberus or the Hobgoblin Leader. Defeating them unlocks higher-tier rewards and progresses the story.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
Don't focus solely on damage. In the early game, movement speed and dash cooldown Blessings are often more valuable for surviving complex boss patterns.
</Callout>
