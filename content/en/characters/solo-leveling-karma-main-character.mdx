<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Blessing System and Roguelite Progression">

To survive the endless waves of the Monarchs' forces, players must master the **Blessing System**. Because the game is structured as a roguelite, every dungeon run begins with a baseline setup that evolves dynamically. As you defeat enemies and clear chambers, you choose randomized Blessings that alter Jinwoo's skills and attributes.

These Blessings allow you to construct specialized builds on the fly, ensuring that no two runs feel identical.
