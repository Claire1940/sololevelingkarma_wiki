<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Blessing System & Build Customization">

The core of the roguelite progression in **solo leveling karma** is the Blessing system. Instead of static, permanent character builds, players collect and combine dynamic Blessings during each individual dungeon run. This ensures that no two runs feel exactly the same.

| Blessing Type | Primary Effect | Combat Impact | Scaling Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Skill Enhancement** | Increases skill levels | Boosts active ability damage and lowers cooldowns | High |
| **Stat Growth** | Enhases core attributes | Improves raw attack power, critical rate, and defense | Medium |
| **Shadow Synergy** | Buffs summoned units | Increases Shadow Army attack frequency and combo damage | High |
| **Utility & Survival** | Utility boosts | Grants health regeneration, faster dash recovery, or shield | Low |

To construct a powerful build during a run, players should follow a structured selection process.

  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starter Weapon">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Skill Enhancements">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Synergize with the Shadow Army">

  </Step>

  </Step>
