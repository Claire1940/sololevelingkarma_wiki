<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Switching and Combat Mechanics">

The combat in *Solo Leveling: KARMA* is fast-paced and requires constant adaptation. The **solo leveling karma karma system guide** emphasizes the "Weapon Switching" mechanic, which allows Jinwoo to carry multiple weapon types and swap them mid-combo. This isn't just for style; different enemies have specific resistances and vulnerabilities that require specific weapon responses.

| Weapon Category | Combat Role | Best Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Daggers / Dual Blades** | Rapid melee DPS | Small, agile Magic Beasts |
| **Greatswords / Hammers** | Heavy impact damage | Shielded enemies and slow bosses |
| **Bows / Long-range** | Distance control | Flying enemies and environmental hazards |
| **Scythes / Special** | Wide AOE clearing | Large groups of low-level mobs |

Effective combat involves rotating through these weapons to maintain pressure. For example, you might use a Greatsword to break a boss's posture, then switch to Daggers to deliver a flurry of critical hits while they are vulnerable.

<Callout type="info" title="Build Diversity">
Remember that your "Karma" Blessings often favor specific weapon types. If you have picked up multiple "Bow Mastery" Blessings, it is more efficient to lean into a ranged playstyle for that specific run, even if your daggers are higher level.
</Callout>
